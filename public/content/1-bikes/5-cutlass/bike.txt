Description: 

The Cutlass is a versatile quick handling singlespeed that can do just about anything. With wide 32c tires and some of the most comfortable grips on the market, the Cutlass is an absolute joy to rally around town.

We've included room and eyelets for fenders because we're from the Northwest and rack mounts for getting stuff off your back. From the frame details, tubing and parts selection, the Cutlass is a unique high quality singlespeed that stands out from the crowd.


##### Features
1. Do-it-all city riding geometry that is comfortable for almost any style of riding 
2. 4130 Butted Seamless Chromoly tubeset and fork that absorbs vibration of the road and creates a smoother ride. 
3. Ability to run fixed gear or singlespeed with flip flop wheel. Hub comes with both a fixed gear cog and a freewheel cog 
4. User friendly flat bar build with flip flop stem to raise or lower bar height. 
5. Puncture resistant Kenda tires that make getting a flat in the city extremely hard so customer doesn't need to work about flatting. 
6. Custom Traitor Segmented Fork with unique segmented fork crown usually only found on high-end custom bikes 
7. Legendary Traitor Copper Metal Headbadge. 
8. Full fender mounts for riding in the rain 
9. Built in dropout chain tensioners for easy wheel alignment and chain tensioning.
